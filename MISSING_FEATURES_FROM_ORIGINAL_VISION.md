**Original Requirement:**
"Detailed stats and analytics about the number of views, likes, shares etc are needed for certain payouts. Everyone that signs up should be monetized correctly and earning money as they learn and grow their platforms."

**What Exists:**
- Bot event logging
- Purchase tracking
- Manual payment tracking

**What's Missing:**

  - Likes, shares, comments aggregated
  - Monetization milestones (e.g., "You're 500 views from $X payout")
  - Revenue projections based on current growth
  - Platform-by-platform performance breakdown
- Payout threshold tracking
- Automatic payout calculations
- Earnings history

## SUMMARY

**Critical Missing Features (Must-Have for Launch):**
1. Multi-platform auto-posting
2. Analytics & monetization tracking dashboard

**High Priority (Should-Have Soon After Launch):**
3. Global monetization guides
4. App store deployment
5. Marketing & promotion strategy
6. Content scheduling & calendar

**Medium Priority (Nice-to-Have):**
7. AI education content expansion
8. Collaboration features

**RECOMMENDATION:**
Focus on #1 and #2 immediately. These were core promises in the original vision and are essential for the "$1M+ in 6 months" revenue goal. Without analytics, creators can't track their progress. Without multi-platform posting, they can't scale efficiently.
