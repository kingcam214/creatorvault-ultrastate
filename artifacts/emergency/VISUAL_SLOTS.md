# CreatorVault Homepage Visual Slots Manifest

**Status:** Emergency recovery manifest for the V6+ video-first homepage.  
**Purpose:** Preserve the homepage direction as a platform-wide visual showcase, not a KingCam-only reel wall. Every slot below must either use verified local media or be replaced by approved Polla AI/OpenArt/Kling/Runway/Replicate motion assets before launch.

## Creative Standard

CreatorVault must read as a **video-first platform OS**. Static images may only act as poster fallbacks, loading states, or visual backups; they are not the primary story. The homepage should communicate that the infrastructure can produce, package, distribute, and monetize almost any creator business format: AI video, music, commerce, apparel, creator promos, brand portraits, clone workflows, platform story, and VaultX monetization.

KingCam remains the **operator/founder proof point**, but the homepage should not feel like a clone-footage page. It should feel like a premium command center where visitors can immediately imagine what their own brand, campaign, drop, funnel, or creator empire could become.

## Hard Rules For Future Sessions

| Rule | Requirement |
|---|---|
| Video-first | Every major above-the-fold or showcase section must lead with motion or motion-ready design. Static assets are only fallbacks. |
| No low-quality posts | No raw text dumps, generic AI flyers, cramped typography, or off-brand visuals should be sent publicly. |
| KingCam balance | KingCam appears as the operator/founder proof point in one or two slots, not as the whole homepage narrative. |
| Business-safe external language | Public external-platform copy must remain business-safe. No adult content generation. |
| Approval gate | No Telegram, WhatsApp, or live promotional send may fire without the exact approval phrase from the user. |
| Verified paths | Homepage media paths must resolve under `client/public/` or use approved uploaded assets copied into the repo. |
| Motion replacement | Any slot marked “static fallback only” must be prioritized for Polla AI motion generation before a polished campaign launch. |
