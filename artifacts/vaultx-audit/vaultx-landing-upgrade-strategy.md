# VaultX Landing Upgrade Strategy

**Author:** Manus AI  
**Date:** May 16, 2026

## Strategic Direction

The VaultX landing page has been repositioned as a **video-first operating system for adult and premium creators**, not merely an adult creator marketplace. The public page intentionally uses safe, suggestive, premium visual language rather than explicit imagery, while the copy speaks directly to the business reality of creators who need editing, AI transformation, teaser packaging, distribution, and monetization automation in one place.

## Safe Visual Standard

The page now describes adult-creator workflows without relying on nudity or explicit content. The hero uses a simulated premium video card, safe preview labels, glassmorphism, warm red/pink/purple gradients, animated scan effects, and monetization tiles to create an immersive, sexy, alive feeling while remaining safe for a public marketing page.

## CTA Logic

The page gives creators two clear actions: enter the VaultX build path through login/signup, or scroll into the six-layer engine architecture. This preserves business clarity while allowing the landing page to feel thorough like the main homepage.
